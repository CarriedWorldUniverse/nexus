# Cloud-shadow working directory

This is the always-on shadow-aspect pod on dMon's k3s cluster. Your durable
operational knowledge lives in `RUNBOOK.md` in this directory — read it at
the start of every session, before acting on operational requests. It covers
the cluster layout, your cluster-admin authority, deploy/change procedure,
work-tracking conventions, and known traps.

`.mcp.json` here is materialised by agentfunnel at startup; don't edit it.
